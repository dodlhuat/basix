Main Content

Click the hamburger icon in the top-left corner to open the push menu. The menu supports nested sub-panels — try clicking "Settings" or "Projects" to drill into sub-levels.

The backdrop overlay closes the menu when clicked. The entire content area shifts to the right when the menu is open.
