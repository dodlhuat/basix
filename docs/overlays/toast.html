Toast

Brief notification messages that appear in the corner of the viewport and auto-dismiss after a configurable delay. Supports four semantic variants and an optional close button.

Variants

Usage

Options

OptionTypeDefaultDescription
contentstring—The notification body text or HTML.
headerstring—Optional title above the content.
markupdefault | success | warning | error'default'Semantic colour variant.
closeablebooleantrueShows a close (×) button.
autoClosenumber—Milliseconds until the toast auto-dismisses. Omit to persist.
