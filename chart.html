Charts

Pure SVG chart components — no external dependencies. Five chart types with animations, tooltips, legends, and full dark mode support.

Line chart with smooth Catmull-Rom bezier curves. Supports multiple series, hover tooltips, and an animated stroke-dashoffset draw effect on load.

Area chart fills the region under the line with a translucent tint. Great for visualising volume or cumulative data over time.

Vertical column chart. Multiple series are rendered side-by-side within each category group. Bars animate with a staggered scaleY grow effect.

Horizontal bar chart — ideal for ranked data or long category labels. Bars animate with a scaleX grow from the left.

Pie chart with per-slice hover pop-out and percentage legend. Slices animate in with a staggered scale + fade.
