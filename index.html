toasts can have an additonal markup to show a colored bar on top: default, success, warning, error.

JAVASCRIPT
toast.show('some content', 'some header', 'success', true, 3000);
 Parameters: content (string | mandatory)
header (string | optional)
markup (enum: default, success, warning, error | optional)
closeable (boolean | optional | default: true)
auto-closure (integer | optional | in ms)
