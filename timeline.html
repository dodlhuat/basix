Project History

October 12, 2023

Project Kickoff

Initial meeting with shareholders to discuss the scope and requirements of the project. Team roles were assigned and the roadmap was drafted.

November 5, 2023

Design Phase Completed

Finalized the UI/UX mockups and received approval from the client. The design system was established with a complete color palette and typography guide.

December 20, 2023

Alpha Release

Released the first playable version of the application to internal testers. Identified major bugs in the navigation logic and fixed critical performance issues.

January 15, 2024

Beta Launch

Opened the platform to a closed beta group of 500 users. Collected feedback regarding the onboarding flow and notification settings.
